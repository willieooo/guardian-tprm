What Guardian TPRM does

Everything your compliance team needs. Nothing they don't.

How it works

From first supplier to board report — in one system

1

Onboard your supplier in 7 steps

Capture service description, PII determination, certifications, ownership, contracts, SLAs, and questionnaire selection. Every field maps to a regulatory requirement.

2

Score and tier automatically

The platform calculates a risk score across 5 dimensions and assigns a tier. Upgrade triggers apply automatically — no manual judgement required.

3

Dispatch questionnaires — suppliers click a link

Suppliers receive an email with a unique portal link. No account needed. They answer, submit, and the score is calculated immediately.

4

Generate your CAP and raise actions

Gap analysis identifies compliance gaps mapped to specific clauses. Generate a branded CAP PDF and raise corrective actions — all in one click.

5

Hand the board a risk register

Generate a board-ready PDF risk register in under 60 seconds. Cover page, executive summary, tier register, SLA performance, open actions, and sign-off page.
